Portal for Networking Company Customers
Mahendra Kumar

Design Challenge

Design a portal for a networking company having cluster of servers across regions that need to be monitored live by customers on different form factors including mobile, desktop and tablets. They are interested in monitoring cluster status, history of downtime, uptime and maintenance schedules.

The Goal

The portal designs should include the wireframes for desktop, mobile and tablet with three levels of interaction.

Research

In the past, I never had an opportunity to design a portal on servers management, so this design exercise was an interesting challenge. I started my research (mostly using internet) to know more about Network administration, servers, and so on. This was important because the portal design should sound relevant and can make some assumptions which can be close to reality, and some knowledge for my thoughts!

This analysis was helpful to understand about different rules being used. And I have made some purposeful assumptions throughout in this design exercise, which are purely based on my understanding.

Concept Designs

Usually, I like to explore many ideas and I prefer the whiteboard or pen/pencil and paper medium—as it is easy, convenient and quick to shape the concepts and evaluate for possible solutions for the problem. Also as part of different ideas exploration, I tried to evaluate different design patterns which could possibly work for the portal design for the customers.
