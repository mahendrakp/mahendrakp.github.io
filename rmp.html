The Challenge

With thousands of TV channels and seemingly unlimited choice of shows and movies, getting to the content that one is looking for is often a challenge. It enforces to juggle multiple remotes to control the home entertainment system and navigate to programming guides to select the choice.

The universal remotes do help to an extend by syncronising the IR codes, but how to take the Harmony Universal Technology to next level of transformational experience?

The Goal

To extend Harmony Universal Remote Technology to next level by removing the barriers between people and their entertainment by replacing confusion of multiple remotes and different program guides using single intuitive touch interface.

Transform the living room entertainment couch experience with Harmony One-Touch Control using smart phones and tablets.
