Health Intelligence

An intelligent analytics product for a complete reporting into enterprise hospital performance.

ENTERPRISE SOLUTION

The product helps to consolidate, analyse both clinical as well as administrative and financial information across multiple facilities in different locations.

The inbuilt data warehouse solution acts as a central repository for all metrics, calculations and dashboard which helps to make informed decisions for planning, delivering better quality of care and increase operational performance.
