Patient Care

A personalised digital touchscreen assistant to help patients track and manage their health, enable ways to access their clinical content and more.

The Goal

To enable a personalized virtual care medium to keep patient and medical professionals connected, especially for chronically ill patients who have been discharged by the hospital but requires continuous regular monitoring on medications, reports, real time vital signs values, etc, to provide care at the bedside of patients anywhere and whenever needed.

The Tablet

The medical professionals have the centralized reliable system to manage patients clinical records including the past history, current and future plan on episodes. The tablet applciation was to enable a personalized virtual connection with their medical professional, equivalent to in-person consultation, to provide care at the bedside of patients.

Also, a patient have plenty of options to know about a particular illness, but are very generic and might be hard to relay or trust - to enable the right knowledge, the help section of the app’s provides the customized curated information by medical professional for their episode of the problem.

The access to medical records, interaction between patient and healthcare professionals were made simple and ease to access, particularly for the chronically ill patients who needs continous monitor after they discharge from hospital or the patients who get care at their bedside. By enabling the personalized interaction, patients engage in their care and that helps them to recover faster to normal life.

The real time syncronization of patients data from the monitoring devices to the centralized system helps the healthcare professionals to pay immediate attention and provide instant care to the patient. Since the patients clicnical datas are centralized, it helps on making better alternate plan and informed decisions for the patients.

App to multitask, capture and present multimedia, including photos, data graphics, video, audio, are helping care providers and patients interact face to face and remotely
